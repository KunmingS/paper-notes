This is a working notebook — half archive, half thinking-out-loud — built from raw paper notes that get distilled into atomic notes and a cross-linked wiki. Inspired by Karpathy's drafts → distill → wiki workflow.

## How it's organized

- **[[wiki/index|🗺 Knowledge map]]** — start here. Topics, clusters, and tags.
- **notes/** — one atomic note per paper, talk, or post.
- **wiki/** — synthesis pages that connect notes across themes.
- **drafts/** — the raw inbox (not published).
