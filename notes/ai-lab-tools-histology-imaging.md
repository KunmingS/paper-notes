# AI tools eating wet-lab histology and imaging workflows

Notes I collected on the shift from hand-curated bench workflows to AI-automated ones in neuroscience labs.

## Brain-slice registration

DeepSlice 等 AI 工具已经能自动配准，速度比人工快 1000 倍以上。

## Cell counting and segmentation

很多实验室还在用 ImageJ 手动数细胞，AI 分割工具（CellPose、StarDist）已经在快速取代手工流程。
